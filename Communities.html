Communities

"Treaty 1 is made up of seven First Nations: Brokenhead, Long Plain, Peguis, Roseau River, Sagkeeng, Sandy Bay, and Swan Lake. These communities have their own histories and cultures but work together to improve their lives and the region. They focus on projects that create jobs, protect the environment, and keep their traditions alive. Naawi-Oodena is one example of this work, aiming to provide homes, businesses, and other services to help both Indigenous and non-Indigenous people live better lives together."
